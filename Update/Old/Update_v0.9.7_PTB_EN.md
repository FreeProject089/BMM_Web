# Welcome to the Public Test Build!

You are running a **pre-release** version of Better Mod Manager. This Public Test Build (PTB) is designed for community testing and feedback.

## The Goal of PTB
The main purpose of the PTB is to:
1.  **Test new features** before they hit the stable release.
2.  **Report bugs** found during your usage.
3.  **Give your feedback** on UI changes and overall experience.

## What's new in PTB v0.9.7

### Performance & Refactoring
- **Instant Mod Detail**: The mod detail panel now loads basic information instantly, with conflict checks running in the background.
- **Selective Conflict Checking**: Drastically reduced lag when activating/deactivating mods by optimizing the conflict resolution engine.
- **Bye-bye Freezes**: New chunked transfer engine ensures your PC stays lag-free even when moving gigabytes of mods.

###  New Performance Engine
- **Disk I/O Limiter**: Take control of your disk speed. Set custom limits for game, mod, and backup drives to keep your system responsive.
- **Performance Dashboard**: Professional-grade PiP monitor for CPU, RAM, and Disk I/O with historical replay and CSV export.
- **Storage Manager**: Deep analysis of your disks, including cloud (Drive, Dropbox, etc.) and network drive detection.
- **Code Refactoring**: Centralized state management and modularized the frontend for better stability.

### UI Polish
- **Dynamic Priority Colors**: Priority labels (MAX, MED, LOW) now have adaptive color intensity based on their numeric value.
- **Double-Click Activation**: You can now double-click on any mod card to quickly toggle its status.
- **Automated Versioning**: Version and static build date are now automatically synced Across all views.
- **Dynamic PTB Badge**: The blue version badge now intelligently reflects the app's configuration.

### Dynamic Internationalization
- **Auto-Detection**: The app now automatically detects new language files added to the Lang folder.
- **Unified Language Selector**: A clean, single dropdown in settings with FlagCDN integration for high-quality flags.
- **Translation Tools**: New buttons in Settings to access the translation guide and copy the JSON template.
